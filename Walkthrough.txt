NOTE: You can always pause the game by pressing escape!!

Walkthrough:
================================================
> Stage 1:
1) Click on one of the bricks on the wall.
2) Click on the button until the lock clicks.

> Stage 2:
1) Take your time
2) Avoid the guards and reach the other door.

> Stage 3:
1) Click on the button until the lock clicks.
2) Choose whatever weapon you prefer.

> Stage 4:
1) Wear the Blindfold, you'll die if you don't.
